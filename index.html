Backend Ruby Development

We're experts in backend Ruby on Rails development. It has proven, time and time again, to be a super productive platform for our development partners' projects. We get more stuff developed, tested, and delivered this way! As Seth Godin would say, let's ship!

Partner with Design Agencies

For Clean, Responsive Look and Feels, we partner with top design/marketing talent. We can work with your designer, but understand Rietta is not itself a design shop. We bring the computer science to add to their human science to provide the best chance for your success.

We Have a Process; Goal is to Ship!

We have a proven development process that has been designed to launch projects to the world, time after time. It starts with discussion your entire project. We work with you to split it into user stories, which are then sorted by technical difficulty and priority, to build a blueprint for your work. We invite you to learn more through
our FAQ.
